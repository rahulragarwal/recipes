Requirements:-
2 cups of cooked rice
vegetables chopped
garam masala(not powdered)
oliev oil,salt,garlic-ginger paste,pepper,chilli powder, turmeric powder.

method:
put three table spoon oil in a pan.
add ginger-garlic paste.
garam masala and turmeric and chilli powder.
add the vegetables and let it cook for certain time
add the cooked rice.
let the rice and the mixture combine.
let the aroma of the spices mix into the vegetables and rice.
when rice starts emitting vapours.
turn the flame of and your biryani is ready.
